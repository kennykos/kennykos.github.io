## Bio

Gabriel is a rising senior studying Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Illinois in Urbana-Champaign. Academically, he is interested in the field of Numerical Methods for Partial Differential Equations, in which he would like to pursue graduate research. In addition to academic studies, Gabe is the president of the Students for Environmental Concerns organization, which orchestrates many sustainability focused projects on campus, such as implementing green rooftops, restricting streets to pedestrian traffic, and hosting community gardens. Outside of school, Gabe is a big baseball fan, closely following the (struggling) White Sox (raised on the north side, but the tickets are cheaper) and the (dominant) Mets (Dad is from Long Island). Other than listening to ball-games on the radio, sifting through baseball references, or playing 16 inch softball (and really any organized sport), Gabe is an avid baker, running a small operation with his roommate that cranks out 55 bagels (the recipe calls for 60, but people like ‘em big) every Thursday and baking pies, tarts, and other pastries throughout the week with his friends. `
